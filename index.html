Selected Projects

MoiréTac — Vision-Through Visuotactile Sensor

Problem: Many tactile skins block vision; tasks like cap removal need line-of-sight + torque + slip/tilt simultaneously.

Approach: Dual-grating optics produce moiré patterns; frequency-domain features + optical flow + physics priors estimate Fz/Tz and contact pose.

Result: Real-time force/torque cues with maintained visual access to the target.

UltraTac — Ultrasound-Enhanced Tactile

Problem: Pure tactile struggles on soft/transparent objects and liquid containers.

Approach: Fuse ultrasound ranging/reflectance with tactile images; temporal features and simple reflectance models.

Result: More reliable material/state classification and steadier manipulation policies.

LED Waveguide Skin — Self-Explaining Tactile

Idea: Embedded LEDs + light-guide for on-sensor status visualization (illumination, contact "hot spots", ROI guides) and easier alignment/debug.

Notes: Brightness uniformity tuning; synchronized prompts with tactile capture.

FlexLock — Variable-Stiffness Arm

Goal: Reversible soft↔rigid switching for tight spaces and human-shared environments.

Method: SMA springs + distributed heating with particle damping; stiffness-trajectory planning with dual-sensor feedback.

Demos: Impact absorption in soft mode; heavy pick/hold after stiffening.
