What problem should the sensing system solve?

Dexterous manipulation fails less because the robot â€œcanâ€™t seeâ€, and more because contact is nonlinear: friction is unknown, occlusion is common, tabletop collisions are real, and small geometry errors amplify. Sensor stack (from minimum viable to â€œrobot skinâ€)

  • Level-0 (default): joint encoders (q/dq), motor current/voltage, temperature/power.
